# Market Validation: Do Things That Don't Scale

## Question
(Implicit — the validation strategy behind the waitlist approach)

## Answer

### The Classic Approach
**Phase 1 — Landing page + waitlist (free)**
Carrd or GitHub Pages for site. Tally/FormSubmit for email collection. Domain if you want it (£5/yr). Share link, see if anyone signs up.

**Phase 2 — Manual MVP**
1. Conduct conversations by hand for 3-5 real people
2. Write cover letters yourself
3. Apply manually
4. Learn what actually matters
5. THEN automate what you learned

### Why This Works
"Raising the question 'what do you hate most about X?' is market research disguised as engagement." The answers directly shape what you build. Most founders build first and discover nobody cares. This flips it.

### Waitlist Mechanics
- Simple form: name, email, industry
- Referral mechanic: "share to move up the list" drives organic growth
- UTM parameters to track where signups come from
