# Competitive Landscape: AI Job Application Market

## Question
I want to turn this into a business with conversational onboarding. Is there any company like this?

## Answer

### Direct Competitors
| Company | What They Do | Gap |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Simplify | Autofills + tracking | Mail merge, no tone |
| Sonara | Automated applying | Bulk, low quality |
| LazyApply | Bulk apply extension | Zero personalisation |
| Kickresume/Resume.io | CV templates | Not AI-native |
| Final Round AI | Interview prep | Different angle |

### The Real Gap
Everything existing is mail-merge with your CV. Cover letters are obviously AI-generated. Recruiters can tell. Tone capture through conversation is genuinely novel.

### Risks
- Job boards block scrapers (LinkedIn, Indeed) — legal/technical headache
- Subscription business needs volume
- Competing with funded companies

### Advantages
- Conversational onboarding is the differentiator
- Founder understands every layer
- Can be profitable at small scale (500 × £20/mo = £10k/mo)
- Can niche down (tech, UK, creative)

### Validation Strategy
1. Landing page + waitlist (free, tells you if anyone wants it)
2. Manual MVP — do it by hand for 3-5 people, learn what matters
3. Automate what you learned
