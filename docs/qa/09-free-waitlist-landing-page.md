# Free Landing Page and Waitlist Options

## Question
Can I do a landing page with waitlist for free?

## Answer

### Free Options Compared
| Service | Free Tier | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Carrd | One-page site | Professional, mobile, built-in email form |
| GitHub Pages | Unlimited | Custom domain, HTML/CSS control |
| Tally.so | Forever free | Form builder, embed or share link |
| Mailchimp | Up to 500 subs | Email list management |
| FormSubmit.co | Forever free | No account, sends to your email |
| Google Forms | Forever free | Ugly but works instantly |

### What Your Landing Page Needs
- Product name
- One sentence: what it does
- 3 bullet points of key benefits
- Email signup
- One line about you as founder (builds trust)

### Domain
- .co.uk on Porkbun or Namecheap: ~£3-5/year
- Worth it for legitimacy

### The Validation Wisdom
A landing page with waitlist is literally market research. Costs nothing. Do this before writing any code.
