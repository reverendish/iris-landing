# Product Naming: Mythology Angle

## Question
Something about personal recruiter. I like mythology.

## Answer

### Shortlisted Names
| Name | Origin | Vibe |
|------|--------|------|
| Iris | Greek | Messenger goddess. Clean, modern, neutral. |
| Mercur | Roman | Fast, clever, slightly techy. |
| Cynosure | Myth term | Guiding star. Premium but hard to spell. |

### Why Iris
Messenger, guide, connects two worlds (you and the job market). One word, four letters, easy to say in any language.

### Domains
- meetiris.co.uk
- heyiris.co.uk

### Runners-up
- Hermes (messenger god, but taken by brand)
- Mercury (Roman, clean but generic)
- Heimdall (Norse watcher, hard to spell)
